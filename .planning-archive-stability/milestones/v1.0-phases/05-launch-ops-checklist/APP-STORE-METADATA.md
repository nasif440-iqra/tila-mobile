## Description

Tila teaches you to read the Quran in Arabic -- starting from zero. Designed for converts, new Muslims, and anyone who wants to connect with the Quran in its original language.

No prior Arabic knowledge needed. Tila takes you from recognizing your first letter to reading connected Arabic script, one step at a time.

How it works:

- Mastery-based learning: Each letter must be mastered before moving on. No skipping, no guessing -- real retention.
- Spaced repetition: Tila brings back letters right when you are about to forget them, building long-term memory.
- Arabic letter recognition: Learn all 28 Arabic letters through visual and audio exercises.
- Connected forms: See how letters change shape when joined together in words.
- Harakat (vowel marks): Learn the diacritics that make Arabic readable.
- Offline-first: Every lesson works without an internet connection. Learn anywhere.

Your learning path:
1. Letter recognition -- identify each of the 28 Arabic letters
2. Letter sounds -- hear and match correct pronunciation
3. Connected forms -- learn how letters join in real Arabic text
4. Quran reading basics -- put it all together

Start with 6 free lessons. Then unlock the full curriculum with a subscription to continue your journey.

Tila is built with care for learners who are just beginning. No assumptions, no shortcuts -- just a clear path from Alif to reading the Quran.

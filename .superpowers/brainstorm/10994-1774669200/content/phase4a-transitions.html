Phase 4a: Transition Patterns

Four transition zones, each with a distinct psychological purpose

1. Lesson Entry — Slide Up

HIGHEST IMPACT

User taps "Start Lesson" on Home → lesson screen slides up from bottom, covering the home screen like a modal sheet.

TRIGGER

Tap "Start Lesson" button

ANIMATION

slide_from_bottom, spring damped

DURATION

~400ms (spring)

LESSON
HOME
↑

2. Lesson Exit — Slide Down Dismiss

REWARD MOMENT

After LessonSummary "Continue" tap → lesson slides back down, revealing Home beneath. Mirrors the entry — what came up goes back down. Satisfying closure.

TRIGGER

Tap "Continue" on summary

ANIMATION

slide_to_bottom, spring damped

DURATION

~350ms (spring)

SUMMARY
HOME
↓

3. Exercise-to-Exercise — Cross-Fade with Slide

FLOW STATE

Between exercises within a lesson: current exercise fades out + slides left, next exercise fades in + slides from right. Creates sense of forward progression without breaking focus.

EXIT

FadeOut + SlideOutLeft (200ms)

ENTER

FadeIn + SlideInRight (300ms)

SLIDE DISTANCE

40px (subtle, not full screen)

4. Onboarding Steps — Directional Flow

FIRST IMPRESSION

Between onboarding steps: current step exits left, next step enters from right. Same "forward progression" metaphor as exercises. Gives the onboarding a sense of journey rather than random screens.

EXIT

FadeOut + SlideOutLeft (250ms)

ENTER

FadeIn + SlideInRight (400ms)

STAGGER

Elements still stagger within step

Implementation Approach

Zones 1 & 2 — expo-router screen options: animation: "slide_from_bottom" on the lesson route. No custom reanimated needed.

Zone 3 — Reanimated entering/exiting props on the exercise wrapper component inside LessonHybrid. Exit left + enter right.

Zone 4 — Reanimated entering/exiting props on the step wrapper inside OnboardingFlow. Same directional pattern as exercises.

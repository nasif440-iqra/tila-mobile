# Privacy Policy

**Tila - Learn Quranic Arabic**

**Last Updated:** April 1, 2026

**Effective Date:** April 1, 2026

## Introduction

Tila ("we," "our," or "the app") is a mobile application that teaches users to read Quranic Arabic. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about the data we collect.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Analytics Data (With Your Consent)

If you grant analytics consent, we collect anonymous usage data through PostHog, a first-party analytics service. This includes:

- **App usage events:** Lessons started, lessons completed, onboarding progress, feature interactions
- **Performance metrics:** Lesson accuracy, duration, streak data
- **Anonymous identifiers:** A randomly generated anonymous ID (not tied to your real identity)

**We do NOT collect:**
- Your name, email address, or contact information
- Advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID)
- Location data
- Device contacts, photos, or files
- Browsing history outside the app

PostHog analytics are **opt-in only**. The app functions fully without analytics enabled.

### 2. Crash Reports (Automatic)

We use Sentry to collect crash reports and error logs. This runs automatically to help us fix bugs and improve stability. Crash reports may include:

- Device type and OS version
- App version
- Stack traces and error messages
- A pseudonymous user ID (the same anonymous ID from analytics, if enabled)

Crash reporting does **not** collect personal information, location, or browsing data.

### 3. Subscription Data

If you purchase a subscription, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives:

- Your anonymous app user ID
- Subscription status (active, expired, trial)
- Purchase transaction identifiers

We do **not** receive or store your payment method, credit card number, or billing address. All payment data is handled by Apple or Google.

### 4. Learning Progress (Stored Locally)

Your lesson progress, mastery data, and streak history are stored **locally on your device** using SQLite. This data is:

- Never transmitted to our servers
- Never shared with third parties
- Fully under your control (you can reset it at any time from the Progress tab)

## How We Use Your Data

| Data Type | Purpose | Legal Basis |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Analytics events | Improve app features and user experience | Consent |
| Crash reports | Fix bugs and prevent crashes | Legitimate interest |
| Subscription status | Deliver premium content you purchased | Contract fulfillment |

## Data Retention

- **Analytics data:** Retained by PostHog according to their data retention policies. You can opt out at any time.
- **Crash reports:** Retained by Sentry for 90 days.
- **Subscription data:** Retained by RevenueCat for the duration of your subscription plus any legally required period.
- **Learning progress:** Stored on your device until you delete the app or reset your progress.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Opt out of analytics:** The app works fully without analytics consent
- **Reset your data:** Use the "Reset All Progress" option in the Progress tab to delete all local learning data
- **Delete your account data:** Contact us to request deletion of any server-side data associated with your anonymous ID
- **Access your data:** Contact us to request a copy of any data we hold

## Children's Privacy

Tila does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or collect identifying information. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us.

## Data Security

- All learning data is stored locally on your device
- Analytics and crash data are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
- We do not maintain user accounts or passwords
- Anonymous IDs cannot be used to identify you personally

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through an in-app notice or by updating the "Last Updated" date above.
